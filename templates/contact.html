  1. What items are prohibited when entering in country?
    • Answer: Here is a list of items that are not allowed in country:
      1. weapons
      2. ammunition
      3. explosive devices
      4. knives greater than 6 inches
      5. vapes, e-cigs, or vape juice
      6. night vision googles
      7. pornographic material
      8. Any drugs or alcohol
      9. Any gambling items (such as rolling dice or poker chips)
      10. For more information please vist the Qatar tourist page: https://visitqatar.com/intl-en/about-qatar
  2. What are the steps I need to do when I enter the country?
    • Answer: Please follow these steps:
      1. Depart off aircraft
      2. Inprocess with PERSCO
      3. Locate your baggage
      4. Dispose of any prohibited items
      5. Immigrate into country with HNCC
      6. Your unit will pick you up from the termianl
      7. Check in at Lodging (ie A6)
      8. Setup Wifi with Babtel
      9. Inspect your new living Qtrs
      10. Finally, go to your assigned Newcomers briefing
